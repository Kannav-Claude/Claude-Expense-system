Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

Spendly collects information in several ways to provide you with the best possible service:

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect your name, email address, password, and any profile information you provide.
  • Financial Data: We collect and store information about your expenses, including amounts, categories, dates, descriptions, and merchant details.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about the device you use to access Spendly, including IP address, browser type, and operating system.
  • Usage Information: We track how you interact with the Service, including features used, pages viewed, and time spent on the platform.
  • Communication Data: If you contact us, we collect information about your communication, including emails and support tickets.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To create and maintain your account and provide access to Spendly
  • To process expense entries and generate financial insights and reports
  • To improve the Service, fix bugs, and develop new features
  • To communicate with you about account updates, security alerts, and policy changes
  • To respond to your support requests and inquiries
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud or unauthorized access
  • To analyze usage patterns and optimize user experience

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share information with trusted third-party vendors who help us operate Spendly, such as cloud hosting providers and payment processors.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government request.
  • Business Transfers: If Spendly is acquired or merged with another company, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.
  • Protection of Rights: We may disclose information to protect the security, rights, or safety of Spendly, our users, or the public.

All service providers are required to maintain confidentiality and use your information solely to provide services on our behalf.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the Service. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at support@spendly.com.

After account deletion, we may retain anonymized or aggregated data for analytical purposes. We will also retain information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes, such as fraud prevention and security.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Spendly uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience and gather usage information:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for core functionality, such as authentication and session management.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with Spendly and identify areas for improvement.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings to improve your experience.

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. Disabling cookies may affect some functionality of Spendly. We do not use cookies for targeted advertising or selling data to third parties.

6. User Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information in your account.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and associated personal data.
  • Data Portability: You may request a copy of your data in a portable format.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of non-essential communications by adjusting your account settings or clicking "unsubscribe" in emails.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@spendly.com.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit using TLS/SSL protocols
  • Encryption of sensitive data at rest
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Restricted access to personal information by authorized personnel
  • Multi-factor authentication options for account access

While we take security seriously, no system is completely immune to breaches. We encourage you to use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication to protect your account.

8. Third-Party Links

Spendly may contain links to third-party websites and services that are not operated by us. This Privacy Policy applies only to information collected through Spendly. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services before providing your personal information to them.

9. Children's Privacy

Spendly is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information and terminate the child's account promptly.

Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided information to Spendly should contact us immediately at support@spendly.com.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the updated policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Your continued use of Spendly after any modifications signifies your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy. If you have concerns about how we use your information, please contact us at support@spendly.com.
